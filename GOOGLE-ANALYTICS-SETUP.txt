GOOGLE ANALYTICS SETUP

The portfolio is ready for Google Analytics 4, but a personal Measurement ID is required.

1. Sign in to Google Analytics.
2. Create a GA4 property for:
   https://azaziro01-glitch.github.io/portfolio/
3. Copy the Measurement ID. It will look like:
   G-XXXXXXXXXX
4. Open index.html.
5. Find the commented Google Analytics block near the end of the <head>.
6. Replace both occurrences of G-XXXXXXXXXX with your real Measurement ID.
7. Remove the HTML comment markers around that block.
8. Upload the updated index.html to GitHub and commit.

Analytics cannot collect data until your real Measurement ID has been added.
